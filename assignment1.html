#1 Song in the US on July 15 2003

Crazy In Love - Beyonce ft. Jay-Z

Starting just 3 days prior on July 12, 2003, "Crazy In Love" took to the top of the US charts. Remaining there for a further 8 weeks, "Crazy In Love" was overtaken for the number 1 spot by "Shake Ya Tailfeather" by Nelly, P. Diddy and Murphy Lee.

Interesting Information on Beyonce's Impact

We all know Beyonce's massive impact on the music industry, and how popular her and her songs are. However, many people may not really consider the massive positive impact she has on the US economy. Economic Analysts believe that Beyonce's "Renaissance" Tour, and Taylor Swift's "Eras" Tour could generate, according to The New York Times, "$4.5 billion in spending" with the "Eras" tour potentially generating 4.6 billion in spending.
